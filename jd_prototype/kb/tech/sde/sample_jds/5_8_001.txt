◆  ROLE SUMMARY
──────────────────────────────────────────────
You will own the development and maintenance of business intelligence solutions, working closely with internal stakeholders and the Tech team to drive data-driven decision-making across Arvind Limited's businesses. As a Software developer in the GCC, you will collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, build, and deploy Power BI reports and dashboards that meet business requirements. Your work will directly impact the company's ability to analyze and act on key performance indicators, driving business growth and optimization.

◆  KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Design and develop scalable data models using star schema and Power BI data modeling capabilities to support complex business reporting requirements
•  Lead the development of Power BI reports and dashboards, leveraging DAX and Power Query (M) to create interactive and dynamic visualizations
•  Drive the implementation of data governance and security standards, ensuring row-level security and access control in Power BI
•  Collaborate with stakeholders to gather requirements and define report specifications, translating business needs into technical solutions
•  Mentor junior team members in best practices for data modeling, Power BI development, and data visualization
•  Own the maintenance and optimization of existing Power BI reports and dashboards, ensuring high performance and data freshness
•  Partner with business analysts to translate KPI requirements into Power BI report specifications, driving business outcomes through data-driven insights

◆  MUST-HAVE SKILLS
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  DAX — creating calculated measures, tables, and columns for data analysis and visualization
•  Power Query (M) — data transformation, filtering, and loading for Power BI reports
•  Data modelling / star schema — designing and implementing scalable data models for business intelligence
•  Power BI Service & Gateway — publishing, managing, and securing Power BI reports and dashboards
•  SQL (query-only) — writing efficient SQL queries for data extraction and analysis
•  Power BI — building enterprise dashboards, creating DAX measures, and implementing row-level security

◆  GOOD TO HAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Microsoft Certified: Data Analyst Associate — demonstrating expertise in data analysis and visualization
•  Experience with data warehousing and ETL tools, such as Azure Synapse or Informatica PowerCenter
•  Knowledge of data governance and security best practices, including data encryption and access control
•  Familiarity with Agile development methodologies and version control systems, such as Git
