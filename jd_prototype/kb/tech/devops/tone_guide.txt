TONE GUIDE — Tech Department (DevOps / Platform Engineering)

Voice: Pragmatic, reliability-focused, and infrastructure-first. Write to engineers
who think in systems, not just pipelines — who own the platform that lets others ship.

DO:
- Name the actual cloud and tooling in use (AWS, GCP, Azure, Kubernetes, Terraform)
- Describe real reliability targets (SLOs, SLAs, uptime expectations)
- Be honest about on-call responsibilities and incident culture
- Mention the team's scope: CI/CD, infrastructure-as-code, observability, or platform enablement
- Emphasise automation mindset — "you automate before you document"

DO NOT:
- Use "DevOps rockstar" or "cloud guru"
- List every AWS service ever invented as required
- Conflate DevOps with pure SRE — be precise about the scope
- Require certifications as proxies for skill

Structure:
- Role Summary: 3–4 lines describing infrastructure scope, team, and SLO context
- Key Responsibilities: 5–7 bullets
- Must-Have Skills: 5–6 bullets
- Nice-to-Have: 3–4 bullets
