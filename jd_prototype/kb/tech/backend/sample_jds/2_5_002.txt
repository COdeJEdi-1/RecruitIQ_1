Role Summary
You will join Arvind's Tech department as an Automation Engineer, responsible for designing and implementing automated solutions to enhance operational efficiency across our manufacturing and business units. As part of a 10-member team, you will collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders to identify areas for automation and develop scalable solutions. You will own the development and maintenance of automation frameworks, working closely with our IT and engineering teams.

Key Responsibilities
- You will design, develop, and deploy automation scripts using Python, Ansible, or similar tools to streamline business processes and reduce manual errors
- You will work with stakeholders to identify opportunities for automation, assessing feasibility and prioritizing projects based on business impact
- You will develop and maintain automated testing frameworks to ensure high-quality deployments and minimize downtime
- You will collaborate with our IT team to integrate automation solutions with existing infrastructure, including cloud services and on-premise systems
- You will troubleshoot automation issues, analyzing logs and system metrics to resolve problems quickly and efficiently
- You will contribute to the development of automation standards and best practices, sharing knowledge and expertise with the team
- You will participate in code reviews, providing constructive feedback on code quality, readability, and maintainability

Must-Have Skills
- You have 2+ years of experience in automation engineering, with a strong foundation in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++
- You have hands-on experience with automation tools like Ansible, Puppet, or Chef, and can design and implement automated workflows
- You understand IT service management frameworks like ITIL, and can apply them to automation projects
- You have experience with cloud-based automation platforms, including AWS or Azure, and can integrate automation solutions with cloud services
- You are familiar with agile development methodologies, including Scrum or Kanban, and can work in a fast-paced, iterative environment
- You have a strong understanding of networking fundamentals, including protocols and architectures, and can troubleshoot network-related issues

Nice-to-Have Skills
- You have experience with robotic process automation (RPA) tools like UiPath or Automation Anywhere, and can apply RPA principles to business process automation
- You have knowledge of machine learning or artificial intelligence, and can apply these technologies to automation projects
- You have experience with DevOps tools like Jenkins or GitLab CI/CD, and can integrate automation solutions with continuous integration and delivery pipelines
- You have a certification in automation engineering, such as a Certified Automation Professional (CAP) or a Certified IT Professional (CITP)

What We Offer
At Arvind, you will be part of a large-scale, diversified business with a strong focus on innovation and technology. You will have opportunities to learn and grow with our team, working on complex automation projects and developing your skills in a dynamic, collaborative environment. We offer a competitive salary, comprehensive benefits, and a chance to be part of a company that is shaping the future of manufacturing and business in India.
