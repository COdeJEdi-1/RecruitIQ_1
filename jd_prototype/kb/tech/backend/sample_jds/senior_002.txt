Senior Backend Engineer — Billing & Subscriptions

Role Summary
You will join our Billing and Subscriptions team as a Senior Backend Engineer, owning the backend systems that manage subscription lifecycle, invoicing, and payment processing for 85,000 paying customers. The team runs Java/Spring Boot services on AWS, integrated with Stripe. You will lead architectural improvements to our billing engine, which is undergoing a migration from a legacy monolith to a set of event-driven microservices.

Key Responsibilities
- You will lead the architecture of the billing engine migration, defining service boundaries, event contracts, and data migration strategy
- You will own the technical quality of the team's output: setting standards, running design reviews, and reviewing every critical PR before merge
- You will work directly with the Finance and Revenue Operations teams to ensure billing accuracy, idempotency, and audit traceability
- You will drive reliability improvements: SLO definition, runbook creation, and chaos testing for critical billing paths
- You will mentor two engineers, providing structured feedback on their technical growth and helping them take on higher-complexity work
- You will own the Stripe integration and evaluate new Stripe capabilities as the team's subject-matter expert
- You will be the primary technical escalation point for billing-related incidents

Must-Have Skills
- You have 5+ years of backend experience, with at least 2 years on billing, payments, or financial systems
- You have deep Java experience (Spring Boot preferred) and can navigate a complex, legacy-adjacent codebase
- You understand idempotency, exactly-once processing, and audit trail requirements for financial systems
- You have worked with Stripe, Braintree, or a comparable payment platform at a deep API level
- You have led a service decomposition or migration project and can articulate what went well and what you would do differently

Nice-to-Have Skills
- You have worked with revenue recognition requirements (ASC 606) and understand how billing system design affects accounting
- You have used AWS EventBridge or Step Functions for orchestrating financial workflows
- You have contributed to PCI-DSS compliance work
- You have experience with multi-currency and multi-tenant billing systems
