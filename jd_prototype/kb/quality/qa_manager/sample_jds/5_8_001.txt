◆  ROLE SUMMARY
──────────────────────────────────────────────
You will own the quality management process for Arvind Limited's textile manufacturing operations, working closely with internal teams, external vendors, and business stakeholders to drive high-quality products that meet customer requirements. As a QA Manager, you will be responsible for managing inline and final inspection processes, conducting customer audits, and reporting quality KPIs to senior management. Your work will have a direct impact on the company's ability to deliver high-quality products to global buyers, ensuring compliance with international quality standards such as ISO 9001 and SA8000.

◆  KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Lead the implementation of quality management systems, including ISO 9001 and SA8000, to ensure compliance with international standards
•  Design and drive inline and final inspection processes using AQL sampling, ensuring that products meet customer requirements
•  Conduct root cause analysis using 8D and Ishikawa methods to identify and resolve quality issues
•  Manage customer audits, including preparation, execution, and follow-up, to ensure compliance with buyer requirements
•  Develop and report quality KPIs to senior management, including defect rates, inspection results, and customer satisfaction
•  Collaborate with production teams to identify and implement process improvements that enhance product quality
•  Mentor junior team members in quality management practices and procedures
•  Own the maintenance of quality records, including inspection results, audit reports, and certification documents

◆  MUST-HAVE SKILLS
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Quality management systems (ISO 9001 / SA8000) — implementation and maintenance
•  Inline & final inspection (AQL sampling) — design and execution
•  Root cause analysis (8D / Ishikawa) — identification and resolution of quality issues
•  Customer audit management — preparation, execution, and follow-up
•  Quality KPI reporting — development and presentation of quality metrics
•  AQL sampling — design and execution of sampling plans
•  ISO 9001 and SA8000 standards — knowledge and application

◆  GOOD TO HAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Six Sigma Green Belt or Black Belt certification — application of lean principles to quality management
•  Experience with third-party lab accreditation (SGS, Bureau Veritas, Intertek) — management of external lab testing
•  Knowledge of chemical testing (RSL/MRSL) — application of chemical testing to quality management
•  Familiarity with Arvind QC standards for denim and woven fabrics — application of company-specific quality standards
•  Experience with QMS implementation (ISO 9001) — design and execution of quality management systems
