Role Summary
You will own data analysis initiatives within Arvind Limited's IT department, focusing on enhancing business intelligence capabilities. As part of a team of 10, you will work closely with stakeholders to identify areas for improvement and develop data-driven solutions. Your work will have a direct impact on Arvind's operations, contributing to informed decision-making across the organization. You will be based in our Ahmedabad office.

Key Responsibilities
* You will build and maintain datasets to support business intelligence initiatives, leveraging your understanding of data structures and analysis techniques.
* Deliver insightful reports and dashboards using Power BI, incorporating filters, slicers, and drill-throughs to facilitate stakeholder engagement.
* You will write DAX measures to calculate key performance indicators such as YTD, MTD, and period-over-period variances, ensuring data accuracy and integrity.
* Coordinate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and develop targeted data visualizations, addressing specific business needs and questions.
* You will diagnose and resolve report performance issues, optimizing data queries and DAX formulas to ensure seamless user experience.
* Develop and maintain documentation for all reports and datasets, outlining data sources, methodology, and intended audience to ensure transparency and reuse.
* You will improve data quality and integrity by identifying and addressing data inconsistencies, working closely with data owners and stakeholders to implement corrective actions.

Must-Have Skills
* You have experience with Power BI, including report design, DAX measures, and data modeling.
* Strong understanding of data analysis principles, including data visualization and storytelling techniques.
* You are familiar with Microsoft Fabric and its applications in data analysis and business intelligence.
* You possess manufacturing or retail domain knowledge, with understanding of key performance indicators such as OEE, OTIF, sell-through, and shrinkage.
* You have excellent stakeholder communication skills, with ability to distill complex data insights into actionable recommendations.
* You are proficient in writing DAX measures, including calculations for YTD, MTD, and period-over-period variances.

Nice-to-Have Skills
* You hold a PL-300 certification, demonstrating expertise in Power BI and data analysis.
* You have experience with Power BI mobile layout design, enabling seamless user experience across devices.
* You are familiar with data pipeline management, including deployment pipelines and data governance.
* You have basic understanding of data science concepts, including machine learning and predictive analytics.

What We Offer
You will be part of Arvind Limited's growing analytics function, with opportunities to work on large-scale data initiatives and collaborate with business leaders. We offer a dynamic work environment, exposure to cutting-edge technologies, and access to learning opportunities through Samarth and Arvind University.
