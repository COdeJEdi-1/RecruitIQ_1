◆  ROLE SUMMARY
──────────────────────────────────────────────
You will own the end-to-end HR Operations function at Arvind Limited GCC, working closely with the HR team, business stakeholders, and external vendors to drive efficient recruitment, onboarding, and offboarding processes. As an HR Operations specialist, you will partner with the DGM to design and implement process improvements, ensuring seamless execution of HR initiatives. Your primary focus will be on full-cycle recruiting, stakeholder management, and HR process optimization, driving business outcomes that support the growth of Arvind Limited's GCC. You will be responsible for managing the recruitment lifecycle, from job posting to onboarding, and ensuring compliance with HR regulations and policies.

◆  KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Manage full-cycle recruiting for various roles, including job posting, candidate sourcing, screening, and selection, using tools like Naukri and LinkedIn Recruiter.
•  Develop and maintain accurate job descriptions, job postings, and recruitment materials, ensuring compliance with HR regulations and policies.
•  Coordinate with stakeholders to understand recruitment requirements, providing regular updates on recruitment progress and ensuring timely closure of open positions.
•  Own the ATS management, ensuring data accuracy, and generating reports to track recruitment metrics, such as time-to-fill, source mix, and offer acceptance rates.
•  Deliver high-quality onboarding and offboarding experiences, ensuring seamless integration of new hires and exit processes for departing employees.
•  Collaborate with the HR team to identify areas for process improvement, implementing changes that enhance the overall HR operations function.
•  Conduct candidate screenings and assessments, ensuring that hiring managers receive high-quality candidates who meet the required job specifications.

◆  MUST-HAVE SKILLS
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  ATS management (Naukri / LinkedIn Recruiter) — managing job postings, candidate sourcing, and application tracking.
•  Full-cycle recruiting — managing the recruitment lifecycle, from job posting to onboarding.
•  JD writing & job posting — developing and maintaining accurate job descriptions and job postings.
•  Candidate screening & assessment — conducting initial screenings and assessments to ensure candidate quality.
•  Stakeholder management — partnering with business stakeholders to understand recruitment requirements and provide regular updates.
•  Onboarding and Offboarding — delivering high-quality onboarding and offboarding experiences, ensuring seamless integration and exit processes.

◆  GOOD TO HAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Certification in HR, such as SHRM-CP or PHR — demonstrating expertise in HR principles and practices.
•  Experience with recruitment marketing tools, such as LinkedIn Recruiter or Glassdoor — enhancing candidate sourcing and recruitment branding.
•  Knowledge of HR analytics and metrics, such as recruitment ROI or time-to-hire — providing data-driven insights to inform HR decisions.
•  Familiarity with HR process automation tools, such as Workday or BambooHR — streamlining HR operations and improving efficiency.

◆  WHAT WE OFFER
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  The opportunity to work with a cutting-edge technology stack, including Naukri, LinkedIn Recruiter, and other HR tools, to drive innovative HR solutions.
•  A dynamic and complex business environment, with opportunities to work on large-scale recruitment projects and drive business growth.
•  A collaborative and cross-functional work environment, with exposure to various business stakeholders and teams, to drive HR process improvements.
