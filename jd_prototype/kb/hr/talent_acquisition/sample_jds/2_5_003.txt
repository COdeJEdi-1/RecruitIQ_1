◆  ROLE SUMMARY
──────────────────────────────────────────────
You will own full-cycle recruiting for various roles within Arvind Limited's GCC, working closely with internal teams, external vendors, and business stakeholders to drive talent acquisition outcomes. As a Talent Acquisition specialist, you will partner with hiring managers to understand their requirements, develop job descriptions, and source top talent using tools like Naukri and LinkedIn Recruiter. Your work will directly impact the growth and success of Arvind's businesses, and you will be responsible for delivering high-quality candidates to meet the company's hiring needs.

◆  KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Manage full-cycle recruiting for various roles, including job posting, candidate sourcing, screening, and selection, using tools like Naukri and LinkedIn Recruiter
•  Develop and maintain relationships with hiring managers to understand their recruiting needs and provide consultative support to ensure successful hires
•  Create and post job descriptions on various job boards and social media platforms to attract top talent
•  Screen and assess candidates to ensure they meet the required qualifications and fit for the role
•  Coordinate interviews with hiring managers and other stakeholders, and provide feedback to candidates on their application status
•  Negotiate job offers with selected candidates and ensure a smooth onboarding process
•  Analyze recruitment metrics to identify areas for improvement and optimize the recruitment process
•  Collaborate with the HR team to ensure compliance with recruitment policies and procedures

◆  MUST-HAVE SKILLS
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  ATS management (Naukri / LinkedIn Recruiter) — managing job postings, candidate applications, and recruitment workflows
•  JD writing & job posting — creating effective job descriptions and posting them on various job boards and social media platforms
•  Candidate screening & assessment — evaluating candidate qualifications, skills, and fit for the role
•  Stakeholder management — building and maintaining relationships with hiring managers and other stakeholders
•  Full-cycle recruiting — managing the entire recruitment process from job posting to onboarding
•  Life cycle management — tracking and managing candidate applications, interviews, and job offers
•  Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) — creating job descriptions, tracking candidate applications, and analyzing recruitment metrics

◆  GOOD TO HAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Certification in recruitment or human resources (e.g. SHRM-CP, PHR) — demonstrating expertise in recruitment principles and practices
•  Experience with candidate CRM tools (e.g. Gem, Beamery) — managing candidate relationships and communication
•  Knowledge of recruitment marketing strategies — attracting top talent through effective job descriptions and social media advertising
•  Familiarity with recruitment analytics tools (e.g. Tableau, Power BI) — analyzing recruitment metrics and optimizing the recruitment process
•  Experience with blue-collar or volume hiring — recruiting for large-scale manufacturing or production environments

◆  WHAT WE OFFER
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  The opportunity to work with a leading textile and manufacturing company, with a diverse range of roles and challenges
•  A dynamic and supportive work environment, with opportunities for professional growth and development
•  Exposure to cutting-edge recruitment tools and technologies, including ATS, CRM, and analytics platforms
