◆  ROLE SUMMARY
──────────────────────────────────────────────
You will own end-to-end talent acquisition for Arvind Limited GCC, driving business outcomes through effective recruitment strategies and stakeholder management. You will work closely with internal teams, including hiring managers and HR business partners, as well as external vendors, such as recruitment agencies and job boards. Your primary focus will be on attracting and hiring top talent to support the growth of Arvind's textile, retail, and diversified businesses. You will be responsible for managing the recruitment process for multiple business units, ensuring timely and high-quality hires.

◆  KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Lead full-cycle recruiting efforts, utilizing tools like Naukri and LinkedIn Recruiter to attract top talent and manage candidate pipelines.
•  Design and implement effective job descriptions and job postings, leveraging your knowledge of the job market and industry trends to drive candidate engagement.
•  Manage and maintain the Applicant Tracking System (ATS), ensuring accurate data entry and candidate tracking.
•  Drive stakeholder management, partnering with hiring managers to understand hiring requirements and develop recruitment strategies.
•  Develop and implement candidate screening and assessment processes, utilizing tools and methodologies to assess candidate fit and potential.
•  Own the recruitment process for multiple business units, ensuring seamless coordination and communication with stakeholders.
•  Mentor junior team members, providing guidance and support to help them develop their recruitment skills and knowledge.

◆  MUST-HAVE SKILLS
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Naukri — job posting and candidate sourcing
•  LinkedIn Recruiter — candidate searching and pipeline management
•  ATS management (Naukri / LinkedIn Recruiter) — managing job postings, candidate sourcing, and applicant tracking
•  JD writing & job posting — developing effective job descriptions and job postings to drive candidate engagement
•  Candidate screening & assessment — utilizing tools and methodologies to assess candidate fit and potential
•  Stakeholder management — collaborating with business stakeholders to understand hiring requirements and develop recruitment strategies
•  Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint) — managing data, creating reports, and developing presentations

◆  GOOD TO HAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Boolean search skills — utilizing advanced search techniques to find top talent on LinkedIn and other job boards
•  Knowledge of recruitment marketing tools, such as Recruitment Marketing Platforms (RMPs) — developing and implementing effective recruitment marketing strategies
•  Certification in recruitment or HR, such as SHRM-CP or HRCI-PHR — demonstrating expertise in recruitment principles and practices
•  Experience with HR technology platforms, such as Workday or BambooHR — streamlining HR processes and improving employee experience
•  Knowledge of Indian labor laws and regulations — ensuring compliance and mitigating risks in recruitment operations
