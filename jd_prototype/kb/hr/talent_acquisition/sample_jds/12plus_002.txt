◆  ROLE SUMMARY
──────────────────────────────────────────────
You will own the overall Human Resources strategy and direction for Arvind Limited GCC, driving business outcomes through effective talent acquisition, management, and development. As the CHRO, you will work closely with the CEO, internal teams, and external vendors to align HR initiatives with business objectives. You will be responsible for leading a team of 50 HR professionals, representing the function in cross-functional forums, and driving the full employee life cycle. Your primary focus will be on building a high-performing organization, fostering a positive work culture, and ensuring regulatory compliance.

◆  KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Define the overall HR strategy and vision for Arvind Limited GCC, aligning with business objectives and driving organizational growth.
•  Lead and manage a team of 50 HR professionals, setting goals, conducting performance reviews, and coaching direct reports to ensure high-performance and career development.
•  Own the full-cycle recruiting process, utilizing tools like Naukri and LinkedIn Recruiter to attract top talent, and developing effective JDs and job postings to drive candidate engagement.
•  Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand hiring requirements, develop recruitment strategies, and ensure seamless onboarding of new employees.
•  Develop and implement comprehensive employee life cycle programs, including talent management, succession planning, and employee engagement initiatives.
•  Represent the HR function in cross-functional forums, stakeholder reviews, and external partnerships, ensuring effective communication and collaboration.
•  Drive process improvements and innovations in HR, leveraging technology and best practices to enhance efficiency, compliance, and employee experience.
•  Manage and analyze HR metrics, providing insights and recommendations to stakeholders on talent acquisition, retention, and development.

◆  MUST-HAVE SKILLS
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Full-cycle recruiting — utilizing tools like Naukri and LinkedIn Recruiter to attract top talent.
•  ATS management (Naukri / LinkedIn Recruiter) — managing job postings, candidate sourcing, and applicant tracking.
•  JD writing & job posting — developing effective job descriptions and job postings to drive candidate engagement.
•  Candidate screening & assessment — utilizing tools and methodologies to assess candidate fit and potential.
•  Stakeholder management — collaborating with business stakeholders to understand hiring requirements and develop recruitment strategies.
•  Full employee life cycle — managing and developing comprehensive programs for talent acquisition, management, and development.

◆  GOOD TO HAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  Certification in HR management, such as SHRM-CP or HRCI-PHR, demonstrating expertise in HR principles and practices.
•  Experience with HR technology platforms, such as Workday or BambooHR, to streamline HR processes and improve employee experience.
•  Knowledge of Indian labor laws and regulations, ensuring compliance and mitigating risks in HR operations.
•  Familiarity with organizational development and design principles, driving business growth through effective talent management and organizational design.

◆  WHAT WE OFFER
──────────────────────────────────────────────
•  The opportunity to lead a high-performing HR team and drive business outcomes through effective talent management and development.
•  Exposure to a complex and dynamic business environment, with opportunities to develop and implement innovative HR solutions.
•  Access to a range of learning and development programs, including training, mentoring, and coaching, to enhance HR skills and knowledge.
