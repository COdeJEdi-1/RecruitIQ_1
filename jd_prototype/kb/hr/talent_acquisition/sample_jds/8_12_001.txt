Role Summary
You will join the HR department at Arvind, overseeing payroll operations for multiple business units across the organization. You will manage a team of 2-3 payroll coordinators, ensuring seamless payroll processing for over 10,000 employees. You will report to the Head of HR and work closely with finance and accounting teams to drive payroll strategy.

Key Responsibilities
- You will own end-to-end payroll management, including salary processing, benefits administration, and compliance with labor laws.
- You will manage payroll budgets, forecasting, and variance analysis to optimize costs and ensure accurate financial reporting.
- You will collaborate with HR Business Partners to develop and implement payroll policies, procedures, and processes that align with business objectives.
- You will lead payroll audits and ensure compliance with internal controls, regulatory requirements, and statutory regulations.
- You will develop and maintain relationships with external payroll vendors, managing contracts and service level agreements to ensure high-quality service delivery.
- You will analyze payroll data and metrics to identify trends, risks, and areas for improvement, providing insights to stakeholders and recommending process enhancements.
- You will manage payroll-related employee queries, resolving issues promptly and ensuring a positive employee experience.

Must-Have Skills
- You have 8+ years of experience in payroll management, with a strong understanding of Indian labor laws, taxation, and regulatory requirements.
- You are proficient in payroll software, such as SAP, Oracle, or similar systems, with experience in payroll processing, reporting, and analytics.
- You have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to build strong relationships with stakeholders, including employees, management, and external vendors.
- You are skilled in budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis, with experience in managing payroll budgets and optimizing costs.
- You have experience in leading a team, with strong leadership and management skills to motivate and guide payroll coordinators.
- You have a strong understanding of payroll best practices, with experience in implementing process improvements and ensuring compliance with internal controls and regulatory requirements.

Nice-to-Have Skills
- You have experience with payroll integration with HR systems, such as HRIS or time-off management systems.
- You have knowledge of international payroll practices and regulations, with experience in managing global payroll operations.
- You have a certification in payroll management, such as a Certified Payroll Professional (CPP) or similar designation.
- You have experience with data analytics and visualization tools, such as Tableau or Power BI, to analyze and present payroll data insights.

What We Offer
You will be part of a large and established organization with a strong reputation, offering opportunities to learn and grow in a dynamic environment. At Arvind, you will have access to a wide range of learning opportunities, from payroll best practices to industry insights, and a collaborative work environment that supports your professional development.
