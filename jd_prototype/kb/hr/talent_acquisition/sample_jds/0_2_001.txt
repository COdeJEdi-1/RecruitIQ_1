Role Summary
You will join the HR department at Arvind as a Talent Acquisition Intern, supporting the recruitment process for various business units. You will work closely with a team of recruiters to learn and contribute to the full-cycle recruiting process. The HR team consists of 10 members, with 2 dedicated recruiters. You will be exposed to a fast-paced environment with a high volume of hiring.

Key Responsibilities
- You will assist in job posting on Naukri, LinkedIn, and Indeed to attract potential candidates.
- You will support resume screening and shortlisting, ensuring the right candidates move forward in the process.
- You will coordinate interview schedules with hiring managers and candidates, ensuring a smooth experience for all parties.
- You will prepare offer letters and assist in the onboarding process for new hires.
- You will maintain accurate records in our ATS, tracking candidate progress and pipeline metrics.
- You will contribute to ad-hoc recruitment projects, such as creating job descriptions or researching new sourcing channels.

Must-Have Skills
- You have a basic understanding of the recruitment process, including job posting and resume screening.
- You are proficient in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel, to manage data and track metrics.
- You have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to interact with candidates and hiring managers.
- You are detail-oriented, with strong organizational skills to manage multiple tasks and priorities.
- You are a quick learner, able to adapt to new systems and processes, such as our ATS.
- You have a strong work ethic, with a willingness to learn and take on new challenges.

Nice-to-Have Skills
- You have experience with social media platforms, such as LinkedIn, to source candidates and promote job openings.
- You have basic knowledge of Boolean search techniques to find candidates on LinkedIn.
- You have experience with pre-screening calls, assessing candidate fit and interest in roles.
- You have familiarity with plant location hiring, particularly in regions such as Gujarat, Jharkhand, Karnataka, or Maharashtra.

What We Offer
You will be part of a large and established organization with a strong reputation, offering opportunities to learn and grow in a dynamic environment. At Arvind, you will have access to a wide range of learning opportunities, from recruitment best practices to industry insights. Our collaborative work environment and supportive team will help you thrive and develop your skills as a Talent Acquisition professional.
