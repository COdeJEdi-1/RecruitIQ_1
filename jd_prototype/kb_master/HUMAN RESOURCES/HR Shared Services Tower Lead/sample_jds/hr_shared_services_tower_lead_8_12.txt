Head of Human Resources – Global Capability Centre (GCC)
Location: Ahmedabad
The Head of HR will lead the people strategy for a new, fast-scaling Global Capability Centre spanning Finance, Technology, Procurement, and HR operations. The role shapes the organisation structure, builds leadership and capability, strengthens culture, and ensures a seamless, high-quality employee experience across the GCC. It partners closely with HR COEs for rewards, policies, and specialised frameworks, while owning the full employee lifecycle within the centre.
Key responsibilities include building a scalable HR operating model; leading talent acquisition and workforce planning; driving performance, development, and engagement; enabling leadership effectiveness; and ensuring disciplined HR governance, compliance, and operational excellence. The role brings people insights, talent intelligence, and organisational judgment into GCC leadership discussions, influencing decisions that shape the centre’s growth and effectiveness.
Success in this position requires strong GCC experience, the ability to operate in a global matrix, and comfort managing both strategic and operational dimensions of HR. The role demands a builder mindset—creating clarity, structure, and capability as the GCC expands.
The ideal profile brings:
•	15–18 years of HR experience with 5+ years leading HR in a GCC
•	Experience scaling centres from early phases to 1000+ employees
•	Strong expertise in HR business partnering, talent management, org design, and operations
•	Ability to work closely with global COEs and navigate complex stakeholder environments
•	A data-driven, execution-focused approach with strong leadership presence
Success in the role reflects in:
•	Smooth scaling of the GCC with strength in talent, culture, and capability
•	High levels of employee and manager experience
•	Mature and reliable HR processes with strong governance
•	Strong global alignment and stakeholder confidence
Tower Lead – HR Shared Services (Employee Lifecycle | GCC)
Reporting to: Head – HR Operations / GCC Head
Location: Ahmedabad
Experience & CTC: 12 to 15 years
Role Purpose
Lead the Employee Lifecycle Shared Services tower, delivering consistent, SLA-driven HR services from hire-to-retire across group companies, while continuously improving experience, efficiency, and controls.
Key Responsibilities
•	Own end-to-end employee lifecycle services: onboarding, lifecycle changes, benefits, exits
•	Run HR service delivery through ticketing, knowledge base, and workflow platforms
•	Ensure SLA adherence, first-time resolution, and employee experience metrics
•	Drive process standardisation and automation across HR transactions
•	Partner with COEs and Business HR on seamless handoffs and clarity of ownership
•	Build dashboards on service quality, volumes, root causes, and productivity
