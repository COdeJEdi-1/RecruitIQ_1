Key Purpose of the Role
To oversee supply chain (quality, production, product development and technical services) operations of:
- Indigo Knitted fabric (through various technologies like circular, jacquard and warp knits) &
- Seamless and full Garment (Shimaseiki) operations like Production, Quality, Procurement and Product Development.
Responsibilities:
Management
- As a member of the core team, contribute to managing complete operations for indigo knit fabrics and seamless to deliver on bottom line performance measures.
- Ensure that targets are met year-on-year basis and work together with stakeholders for smooth operations.
Production
- Manage production activities through better planning in order to ascertain viability of production plans.
- Ensure timely conduct of the daily meeting and discuss/follow up issues until resolved.
- Investigate/review all operations, identify failures and ensure implementing of permanent corrective actions.
- Contribute to the improvement of process through continuous improvement initiatives.
- Analyze training needs for special operations/attachment requirements and take actions to fulfil the requirements.
- Ensure accurate and timely reporting of output to management via reports.
Quality
- Set up and accreditation of knits lab.
- Set up the technical service department.
- Manage and ensure implementation of quality / control systems / processes in order to meet product / process quality requirements.
- Manage the quality assurance department, set targets in line with plans, plan work, obtain feedback, address issues, and allocate resources pertaining to all quality assurance activities to facilitate smooth operations.
- Monitor the conduct of all audits, implement, maintain, review and improve all quality assurance and control systems / processes to ensure product / process compliance with customer requirements.
- Liaise with relevant personnel including customers and suppliers, make decisions such as evaluating feasibility of new embellishment plants, non-conformity, taking customer standards / requirements, plant capability / standards, commercial acceptability into account and implement systems to prevent reoccurrence of such issues.
Procurement
- Responsible for the development, implementation and on-going management of the Procurement Strategy.
- Lead the development and implementation of procurement policies and system strategies to support tendering, contracts management, spend analytics and supplier performance management.
- Drive continuous improvement, best value and quality improvements within the team and departments, challenging existing practices and actively seeking ways to achieve better outcomes, contributing to ensure key performance and financial targets are met and corrective action taken where necessary.
Product Development
- Developing new products or improvement of existing products with thrust on reducing cost.
- Developing fabrics that are in line with the strategic vision of the Company.
- Develop innovation pipeline with new performance materials that deliver unique customer experiences on performance, delivery, and cost.
- New edge technology upgradation or introduction.
- Drive development & fabric delivery timings with a clear understanding of the development cycle and production strategy.
- Build on existing expertise in all areas of fabrics processing with a focus on best in class. Learn and apply environmental knowledge to fabric knitting & processing selection.
Skill sets and experience:
- Should have around 20 years of experience in Knit Fabric & Garmenting Operations.
