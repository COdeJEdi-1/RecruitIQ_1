Business Profile:
Our telecom business reflects the tenacity that defines us. Starting small 25 years ago, we are market leaders today in the telecommunications business in Mini-EPABXs for the SOHO, small & medium businesses. Our partnership with NEC, Alcatel Lucent Enterprise, Cisco, Polycom for India is enabling unified communication, collaboration & video conferencing solutions for SMBs and large enterprises. Our Shared Mobile Radio Service and GPS based vehicle tracking systems and Fleet Management Systems are powering movement for industries that are driving India's growth story.
- Bespoke analytics and system integration capabilities.
- E2E ground transportation of people, Passenger Information System, Taxi Management System, Dial 100 for Public Safety, Cold Chain logistics, Vehicle Diagnostics, Mining, Oil & Gas transportation, Hazardous Chemicals transportation — all with user & driver facing mobile apps.
- India's largest Public Mobile Radio Trunking Services operator with a market share in excess of 90%.
- Intrinsically safe Walky Talky devices for highly inflammable environments with unlimited talk Groups, campus & citywide coverage and high network uptime.
A leading SMB Distribution Focused Company selling Telecom products and VC solutions through its nationwide network of Channel partners is looking to enter the Security and Surveillance Industry (CCTV Cameras, DVRs & NVRs) in India.
The Company wishes to add to its senior management team a seasoned professional with a proven track record of more than 15 years in the Security & Surveillance Industry, in a Business Development role with an in-depth knowledge of success requirements of establishing and growing this business for a potential new entrant, including but not limited to product sourcing, positioning and building a robust distribution eco system from the ground up, in the face of firmly entrenched large competitors.
The job shall entail strategizing the Company's entry and profitable growth in the Security and Surveillance Industry by taking a call on:
- Selection of products to be introduced and key differentiators.
- Selection of sourcing partners for the chosen products.
- Decision on whether to use current brand name (in a line extension mode) or choose a new brand name.
- Single or multi-tier distribution architecture.
- Positioning strategy and meeting success requirements to enroll new Distribution Partners.
- Sales, service and marketing organization.
- Local manufacturing or trading.
- Business plan and resource requirements for first 3 years.
Location: Pune, Maharashtra.
