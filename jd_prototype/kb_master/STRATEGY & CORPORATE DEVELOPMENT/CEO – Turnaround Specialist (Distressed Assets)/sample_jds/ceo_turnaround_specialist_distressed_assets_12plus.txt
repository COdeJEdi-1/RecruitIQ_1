We are one.
Yet, we are many.
We are a lifestyle conglomerate that’s also building low cost homes. A pioneer of denim that’s pioneering sanitation solutions. We are a global apparel leader that’s driving new age agricultural practices. A wearable technology manufacturer that’s also supporting economic development of tribal women. A state-of-the-art protective gear designer that’s transforming water management.
That’s where a 1.4 billion dollar institution stands today. Where we aren’t just driven by bottom lines and profits, but want to drive social impact. Where our CSR and sustainability initiatives are slowly but surely changing lives and making a difference to thousands. From being just a textile company, we’ve come a long way and are striving every day to create opportunities where none exist.  42,000 employees across verticals ranging from retail, advanced materials and environmental solutions to real estate, apparel and agriculture, believe that at Arvind, the possibilities are endless. It just depends on how you fashion them. It’s this philosophy that has helped us touch people’s lives through innovative and pioneering solutions since 1931. And it’s this philosophy that continues to drive us to change tomorrow for the better.
Welcome to Arvind.
Welcome to a world of fashioning possibilities.
The Scope of the Assignment
Confidentiality will be maintained at every stage of our search processes.
Key Result Areas:
The incumbent will be involved in identifying the distressed assets in textile domain and will offer a new set of eyes, skills and understanding of troubled situations to independently evaluate the distressed assets’ present circumstances.
The incumbent will be involved in preparing the current state of the distressed assets.
The incumbent will be instrumental in preparing the business plan outlining and suggesting possible courses of action.
The incumbent will be involved in implementing the business plan and also involve in building the team both inside the company and from outside resources.
The incumbent must keep vigil over the plan and monitor the business plan – analyze the variances to determine their causes and the validity of the underlying assumptions.
The incumbent will focus on cash flow – he will stabilize the cash flow and perform a quick analysis of the company's sales and profit centers and of its asset utilization.
The incumbent will focus on rebuilding the credibility and confidence with lenders, trade suppliers, employees, customers, shareholders, and even the local community at large. He should serve as a liaison or intermediary with these outside constituencies to calm troubled waters and to present bad news as a preamble to a plan for recovery.
Desired Candidate Profile
The incumbent should be a management graduate with minimum 20 years of experience in a manufacturing Company - currently working as a Business Head with a minimum of 02 turnaround experiences from his earlier companies.
The incumbent should offer new ideas, skills and understanding of troubled situations to independently evaluate the company's circumstances.
The incumbent must have good understanding of financial ratios – the ‘Z Score’ attempt to evaluate a distressed manufacturing Company.
Must be a goal oriented professional and capable enough to take hard decisions.
The incumbent should be an experienced negotiator with creditors to whom he tells the truth, be it good or bad and relies upon his credibility to build the consensus necessary to build for the future.
The incumbent must bring a new set of ideas to a situation as well as an experienced and knowledge base of managing businesses through the turnaround processes.
The incumbent should bring to the table his management ability; his ability to marshal resources and maximize the value from those diverse resources.

The Scope of the Assignment
Arvind wishes to acquire some distressed assets in textile domain and for this they would like to hire a turnaround specialist who will identify, do a proper due diligence, acquire distressed assets and work in tandem with the promoters/ senior management team of Arvind Ltd. to turnaround the distressed assets to a profitable enterprises. The assignment will be to facilitate the process of mapping such specialist  - A business leader who knows how to spot a problem and understand what needs to be done to fix it – a turnaround specialist who has a good grounding in management of basics, a firm grasp of financial principles, a sharp eye and, most importantly, an intuitive understanding of what is wrong with a particular company. The incumbent will act as a catalyst to recover the distressed assets to a viable one.
