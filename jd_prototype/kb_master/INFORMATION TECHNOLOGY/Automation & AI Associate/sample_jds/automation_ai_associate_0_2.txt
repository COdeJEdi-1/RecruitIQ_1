Job Description: Automation & AI Associate
Location
Ahmedabad
Role Objective:
Build and deploy automation and AI-driven solutions that directly improve business efficiency, reduce manual effort, and enable scalable operations across functions.
This is a hands-on builder role, not coordination, not reporting, not PPT.
Key Responsibilities
Design, build, and deploy automation workflows across business functions (Finance, HR, Procurement, Ops, Business teams)
Develop Python-based scripts/tools for process automation, data handling, and integrations
Build and experiment with AI-led use cases (LLMs, copilots, agents, internal tools)
Use platforms like:
Microsoft Power Platform (Power Apps, Power Automate)
RPA tools (Automation Anywhere / UiPath / equivalent)
Rapidly prototype and ship internal products/tools
Integrate APIs, SaaS tools, and internal systems to create end-to-end automation
Identify manual processes and convert them into scalable automation solutions
Work directly with business stakeholders to translate problems into working solutions
What Success Looks Like
You ship working automations, not concepts
You reduce manual effort and turnaround time measurably
You build reusable tools/products, not one-off hacks
You operate with speed and ownership, not dependency
Core Skills Required
Strong hands-on capability in Python scripting
Experience with automation tools:
Power Automate / Power Apps
RPA tools (Automation Anywhere, UiPath, etc.)
Working knowledge of:
APIs & integrations
Data handling (Excel, SQL, basic data pipelines)
Exposure to AI tools / LLMs / automation frameworks
OpenAI, Azure AI, or similar ecosystems
Ability to build prototypes quickly and iterate
Profile We Are Looking For
1 – 3 years of experience
Background irrelevant (Tech, Ops, Consulting, Startup, etc.)
Must demonstrate:
Builder mindset (has created something real)
High ownership (doesn’t wait for instructions)
Bias for action (ships fast, learns faster)
Comfortable working in zero-structure environments
What We Do NOT Want
Pure support engineers
Only certification-driven profiles
People who “know tools” but have never built anything meaningful
Slow executors who need constant direction
Why This Role Matters
This team will form the core of our Automation & AI capability within the GCC, driving productivity, cost optimization, and scalable digital operations.
