Central Head Administration and Facility Management :
Key Purpose of the Job
Shall be responsible for managing wide array of facility management functions including Operation & Maintenance, Housekeeping, Security, Cafeteria, Vendor Development, Purchase, Transport, Waste Management etc.; demonstrated capabilities in effecting business process improvements and implementing cost rationalization measures to enhance operational efficiency.
Major responsibilities:
Handling operation & maintenance of the office premises.
Managing the overall maintenance of facilities & equipments like UPS, IBMS, Access control, CCTV, kitchen equipments, Wind Solar hybrid systems, solar water heating systems, solar BIPV system, Rain water harvesting, water & sewage treatment plants, RO system, organic waste composting, fire fighting & alarm system, fire suppression system etc.
Ensuring proper work space planning and allocation.
Managing Help Desk & Reception, Fire & Safety, Security, Transport, Travel, VISA, Passport,  Telephone Set, EPBX system,  Cafeteria Management, Event Management, Mail Desk etc. and Housekeeping operations
Establishing systems & processes for improving effectiveness of the function by maintaining compliance through cost, quality & delivery
Interacting with departments for executing maintenance of equipments/ timely delivery of new equipment and enhancing overall efficiency and customer centricity.
Inspecting field sites / facilities to observe and evaluate condition to ensure general up-keep and maintenance of premises / equipments/ appliance / furniture / furnishings / vehicles etc.
Spearheading transport system viz., OTA/ No shows tracking/logging/checking/routing.
Facilities to manage : Textile and garment Operations of Arvind Ltd based in Ahmedabad and Bangalore, Gujarat, Corporate Office, Envisol

Internal: Business CEO and HODs , BU – HR , Finance, Accounts, IT Applications Etc.
External: Vendors, Suppliers, Government bodies .
